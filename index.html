What is DSCET SparkTech Club?

DSCET SparkTech Club is community groups for students interested in Google developer technologies. At DSCET SparkTech Club, we strive to create an environment where innovation and learning thrive.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between theory and practical application, providing a platform for students to grow as developers and leaders.
