Tech and Non-Tech Symposiums at DSCET, Mamallapuram

Dhanalakshmi Srinivasan College of Engineering and Technology aims to create an inclusive and enriching learning ecosystem where students can explore their passions, collaborate with peers, and make meaningful contributions to society. Join us as we embark on a journey of exploration, innovation, and growth.

Being behind the lens

Description: Being behind the lens is a technical event where participants explore the art and science of photography, learning various techniques and practices to capture compelling images.

Details:
  • Learn fundamental principles of photography, including composition, lighting, and exposure.
  • Practice using different camera settings and equipment to achieve desired effects.
  • Participate in photo walks and sessions to apply techniques in real-world scenarios.
  • Receive feedback and guidance from experienced photographers to improve your skills.

Want ad

Description: Want Ad is a creative advertising event where participants conceptualize and create advertisements for products, services, or causes.

Instructions:
  • Participants will be assigned a product, service, or cause to create an advertisement for.
  • Develop a creative concept for your advertisement, considering the target audience and objectives.
  • Design your advertisement using any medium, such as posters, videos, or digital graphics.
  • Submit your completed advertisements through the provided online platform or designated submission portal.
  • Advertisements will be judged based on creativity, effectiveness, and adherence to the given assignment.

Bioscope

Description: Bioscope is a cinematic event featuring screenings of short films, discussions on filmmaking techniques, and workshops on scriptwriting and directing.

Instructions:
  • Attend screenings of short films curated for their creativity and storytelling.
  • Engage in discussions with filmmakers and industry professionals on various aspects of filmmaking.
  • Participate in workshops to learn about scriptwriting, directing, and other essential skills for filmmakers.
  • Network with fellow film enthusiasts and professionals to share ideas and collaborate on future projects.
