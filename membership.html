Membership

Thank you for your interest in becoming a member of DSCET SparkTech Club! Membership is open to all students who are passionate about Google developer technologies and innovation.

To become a member, simply fill out the membership application form below and submit it. Once your application is approved, you will officially become a member of our club!

Membership Benefits and Perks

  • Access to exclusive workshops, seminars, and training sessions
  • Networking opportunities with industry professionals and fellow enthusiasts
  • Opportunities to work on collaborative projects and hackathons
  • Discounts on club merchandise and event tickets
  • Recognition for your contributions and achievements
