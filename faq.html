Frequently Asked Questions for DSCET SparkTech Club

Answers to common questions


DSCET SparkTech Club is a student organization at DSCET dedicated to fostering technological innovation and providing a platform for students to explore and develop their technical skills.

Membership information and enrollment procedures can be found on our website or by contacting club officials directly.

DSCET SparkTech Club organizes a variety of events such as workshops, hackathons, seminars, and networking sessions to promote learning and collaboration among members.

You can reach out to us via email, phone, or visit our club office during operating hours for any assistance or inquiries.

No, there is no fee required for joining DSCET SparkTech Club. Membership is open to all students.

Joining DSCET SparkTech Club provides opportunities for networking, skill development, project collaboration, and participation in tech-related events and competitions.

DSCET SparkTech Club organizes events throughout the academic year, with the frequency varying depending on the nature and scale of the event.

Yes, DSCET SparkTech Club welcomes students from all disciplines who have an interest in technology and innovation.
